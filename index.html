SBJITMR ACM Student Chapter

The Association for Computing Machinery (ACM) stands as a global scholarly society dedicated to the field of computing, with a rich history dating back to its establishment in 1947. Recognized as the world's largest scientific and educational computing society, ACM has played a pivotal role in shaping the landscape of computing. In alignment with this prestigious legacy, ACM-S.B. Jain was founded in 2023 by the Department of Emerging Technologies at S. B. Jain Institute of Technology, Management and Research, Nagpur. Since 2023, it has been jointly managed by the departments of Emerging Technologies AIML-AIDS. ACM-S.B. Jain serves as a dynamic professional community, committed to fostering awareness of the latest technological advancements and providing educational insights. With a focus on nurturing aspiring change-makers, the association organizes events centered around cutting-edge technologies, contributing to the holistic development of students. Join us on a journey of technological exploration and educational excellence!

From HOD's Desk

Dr. Hemant Turkar

Dear Students, Faculty, Staff, and Visitors,

Welcome to the SBJITMR Student Chapter of the Association for Computing Machinery (ACM). I am pleased to welcome you all to the Department Of Emerging Technologies and I extend my warmest greetings to you all. True to our name, we represent the cutting edge of the 21st century. With specialized branches of CSE in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning & Data Science, we are dedicated to continually enhancing the skill sets of our students. Through carefully designed programs and practical experiences, we ensure that our students are well-prepared to excel in their prospective professional journeys. At our department, we strive to integrate quality education with a focus on upcoming technologies, essential for placing competent students in the IT industry. Providing guidance and support, we aim to shape our students for success. But when we talk about education it also comprises extracurricular activities so, beyond academics, we recognized the importance of extracurricular activities in building confidence and preparing students for a bright future. Our department hosts frequent events such as “ACM-ICPC and Smart India Hackathon” that contribute to our integrated development program. In conclusion, we also focus on nurturing students socially. I emphasize my role in transforming students into both technically and socially competent individuals, ready to open doors to success and a promising future. I invite all of you to collaborate with us in shaping tomorrow's world with today's youth. They will lead us to technological advancements to the greatest extent.


Faculty Co-ordinator's Desk




Prof. Ravi V. Asati

Prof. Ravi V. Asati, an accomplished Assistant Professor, holds a Master's in ME(WCC), a Post Graduate Diploma in C-DAC (PGDAC), and a Bachelor's in Computer Technology (B.E in CT), accumulating over 15 years of valuable experience in academia. His expertise spans crucial areas such as Computer and Network Security, as well as Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning (AIML). As an educator, Prof. Asati is dedicated to shaping the minds of aspiring technologists, utilizing innovative teaching methodologies and mentorship. Actively engaged in research, he contributes to advancements in cybersecurity and AI.
For inquiries, mentorship, or further information, feel free to contact him at raviasati@sbjit.edu.in.







Prof. Yogesh Narekar

Prof. Yogesh Narekar, a seasoned Assistant Professor with over 14 years of experience, holds an M.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering. Armed with a wealth of knowledge, he brings a comprehensive understanding of crucial areas such as Programming Languages, Data Structures, and Operating Systems. Prof. Narekar's commitment to academic excellence is reflected in his dedicated approach to the foundational aspects of computer science education. His passion for fostering learning environments goes beyond the classroom, making him an approachable resource for inquiries or to initiate enlightening discussions.
For inquiries, mentorship, or further information, feel free to contact him at yogeshnarekar@sbjit.edu.in.
