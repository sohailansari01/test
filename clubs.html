   Student Clubs

With an objective of help students to pursue their hobbies and interests and to bring people together the student clubs are created at the Institute. As a club member one knows about one's self, one's interests and one's goals. Students can unravel their strengths like multitasking, organization skills, team Building skills, leadership skills and service mindedness. They can also add skills to their repertoire. The clubs also help in pursuing an old hobby. Networking opportunities being major benefit Club members develop bond among themselves which help in growing their careers. Association of students with clubs help in showcasing ones domain interest, balance between work and hobbies. Activities organized/participated as a club member looks good on the resume.

Clubs


  • Sports Club

    The College Sports Club is a dynamic community fostering athleticism, teamwork, and leadership through a variety of competitive and recreational sports. We provide students with opportunities to excel in their favorite sports, build lasting friendships, and promote a healthy, active lifestyle.


  • Technical Club

    The Technical Club empowers students to innovate and excel in cutting-edge technology and engineering projects. We offer hands-on experiences, workshops, and collaborative opportunities to turn ideas into impactful solutions.


  • Management Club

    The Management Club cultivates leadership and business acumen through interactive workshops, networking events, and real-world project experiences. We equip students with the skills and insights to excel in dynamic and competitive professional environments.


  • Esports Club

    The Esports Club unites gaming enthusiasts to compete, collaborate, and thrive in the exciting world of competitive gaming. We provide a platform for skill development, team play, and strategic growth across various popular game titles.


  • Cultural Club

    The Cultural Club celebrates diversity and creativity through vibrant events, performances, and artistic expressions. We bring together students from all backgrounds to share, learn, and showcase the rich tapestry of global cultures.
