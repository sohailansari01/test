Online Faculty Development Program On
“AI and Machine Learning”

The AI and machine learning domain is a rapidly evolving field at the intersection of computer science, mathematics, and statistics. It encompasses the development of algorithms and models that enable computers to learn from data and make intelligent decisions without explicit programming. From natural language processing and computer vision to predictive analytics and autonomous systems, AI and machine learning technologies are revolutionizing industries and transforming the way we interact with technology. As research continues to advance, the potential for innovation and impact across various domains, including healthcare, finance, transportation, and beyond, is boundless. This FDP on "AI and Machine Learning" serves as a platform to empower educators with the knowledge, skills, and resources necessary to effectively teach and integrate these transformative technologies into academic curricula. Through interactive sessions, workshops, and hands-on exercises, participants will gain a comprehensive understanding of AI and machine learning concepts, methodologies, and applications. It is designed to equip educators with the knowledge, skills, and resources needed to effectively teach and integrate AI and machine learning concepts into their curriculum.

About Department of Emerging Technologies

Dedicated to its mission of delivering exceptional technical education and fostering the development of adept professionals, the Department of Emerging Technologies was established at the outset of the academic year 2021-22. At the heart of this initiative is a commitment to excellence, driven by a vision to prepare students for the ever-evolving landscape of technology. With state-of-the-art infrastructure at our disposal, we have created an environment conducive to learning, innovation, and skill development. Our department is led by a team of highly qualified and experienced faculty members who bring a wealth of knowledge and industry insights to the learning process. Through their guidance, mentorship, and hands-on approach to education, students are not only equipped with theoretical knowledge but are also immersed in practical applications, ensuring a holistic and comprehensive learning experience. Offering undergraduate programs with an intake of 120 in Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning (AI & ML) and Artificial Intelligence & Data Science (AI & DS), we provide a robust curriculum that aligns with the latest industry trends. The curriculum is designed to empower students with the skills and knowledge required to thrive in these dynamic fields, emphasizing practical applications and real-world scenarios. As a department, we are dedicated to staying at the forefront of technological advancements. Our focus extends beyond traditional learning, as we actively engage in exploring cutting-edge advancements and innovations in AI, ML, and Data Science. Through strategic collaborations, industry partnerships, and research initiatives, we aim to contribute to the ongoing evolution of these fields while ensuring our students are well-prepared for the challenges and opportunities that lie ahead. Join us on this exciting journey of discovery and innovation, where the future of technology unfolds in the hands of our aspiring professionals.
